Founded in 2006 by Jonathan Hill, The Northern Block is a collaborative type foundry internationally recognised for producing modernist fonts for storytellers and creatives. The Northern Block's highly skilled and enthusiastic team designs and develops typefaces with creatives around the world, providing mentoring, technical and creative support. Today the company is recognised for producing typefaces fit for a modern purpose and is pushing forward the design of non-latin scripts, including Arabic, Cyrillic and Greek.
